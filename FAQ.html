Frequently asked questions

General question

What is Rentra?

The Rentra is a tool designed to help students and professionals find paying guest (PG) accommodations that are close to their university or place of study. It provides a convenient way to locate and compare available options based on proximity and other criteria.

How does Rentra work?

Users enter their university name or address into Rentra search. The tool then searches for available PG accommodations within a specified radius around the university. It displays a list of options, complete with details like rent, facilities, and contact information.

Is Rentra service free?

Yes, the basic search functionality of Rentra is free to use. However, there may be premium features or additional services that could incur a fee.

How accurate is the proximity information?

The proximity information is based on the address or coordinates provided for the university and the listed PG accommodations. We strive for accuracy, but actual travel time may vary depending on traffic and specific routes.

Can I list my own PG accommodation on the Rentra?

Yes, property owners or managers can list their PG accommodations on Rentra. Contact our support team or visit our website for information on how to add your listing and the requirements for doing so.

Inquiry question

Can I filter search results based on specific requirements?

Yes, Rentra allows you to filter search results based on various criteria such as rent range, amenities, room type, and more. This helps you find accommodations that best meet your needs.

How often is the accommodation data updated?

Accommodation data is updated regularly to ensure that the information is current. However, availability can change quickly, so we recommend contacting the property directly for the most up-to-date information.

Can I contact the property owners directly through Rentra?

Rentra provides contact information for property owners or managers, allowing you to reach out to them directly. Some listings may also include a contact form or booking request feature.
